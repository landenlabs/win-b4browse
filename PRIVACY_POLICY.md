Privacy Policy for B4-Browse

Effective date: 2026-06-21
Version: 1.0


1. Summary

B4-Browse (B4Browse) (the "App") by LanDen Labs is a desktop utility that inspects local system and browser configuration to help users verify their environment before launching a browser. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how that information is used, stored, and protected, and what control you have over your information.

2. Information the App Accesses

- Local system information: OS version, installed programs' metadata, file paths when examining program launch/activity (e.g., to show where a binary runs from).
- Local diagnostic data: optional local logs and indexes the App reads for safety checks (examples: Windows UserAssist, Windows Search app index, SRUM DB) when available and when the app has the required permissions.
- Browser configuration: Chrome/Chromium profile settings and counts of extensions, saved-password counts (counts only, not password contents). The App does not read or transmit saved passwords or other secrets.
- Optional user-provided artifacts: files or logs you explicitly choose to attach when using a "Send report" or support feature.

3. How the App Uses the Information

- All analysis is performed locally on your device to produce UI reports and recommended actions.
- The App does not transmit any system, browser, or usage data off the device by default.
- If you explicitly choose to send a report (for support or diagnostics), the App will only transmit the data you confirm. The App will show what will be sent and request explicit consent before transmission.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

- The App does not share or sell personal data to third parties.
- Third-party libraries or components included in the App (e.g., for packaging or runtime) are used purely locally. If you configure any telemetry or analytics SDK, that will be disclosed here and require your consent.

5. Telemetry and Analytics

- By default, telemetry/usage analytics are disabled. If you enable anonymous usage reporting in the App settings, lightweight telemetry may be sent to the specified service to help improve the product. Telemetry, when enabled, excludes personally-identifying content unless you explicitly include it in a support report.

6. Data Retention

- The App does not create a remote account or maintain cloud-stored personal profiles.
- Any diagnostic or temporary files created by the App on your device are retained only for the duration necessary to perform the requested operation and are removed where reasonably possible.

7. Data Security

- The App follows reasonable security practices for local processing and for any data you choose to send. If you send reports, transport is secured using HTTPS/TLS. You are responsible for reviewing and redacting any sensitive information before sending if desired.

8. Children

- The App is not directed to children under 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA and similar)

- Access: You can view the data the App displays locally on your device.
- Deletion: Because data is processed locally and not uploaded by default, deletion is typically a matter of uninstalling the App and removing app-created files. If you have sent a report, contact us at LanDenLabs@gmail.com to request deletion of that report.
- Opt-out: You may opt out of any optional telemetry or diagnostic submissions at any time via the App settings.
